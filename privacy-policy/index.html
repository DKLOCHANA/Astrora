Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Section 01

Introduction

Welcome to Astrune. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered astrology and self-discovery application.

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Section 02

Information We Collect

a. Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (securely encrypted)

b. Birth & Chart Data

To generate your natal chart and personalised readings, we collect:

  • Your birth date, birth time, and birth place (or a solar-chart fallback if your exact birth time is unknown)
  • Charts you save for partners, friends, or family for compatibility readings
  • Placements and transits you favourite or revisit

c. Journal & Reflection Data

When you use the daily companion, we may store:

  • Your journal entries and reflective prompt responses
  • Optional mood logs
  • Your daily scan/reading streak and the date of your most recent reading

d. Subscription and Payment Information

Subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store via RevenueCat. We do not store or access your payment details — payment data is handled securely by Apple.

Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Compute your natal chart and generate personalised AI daily readings based on real transits
  • Generate compatibility (synastry) readings between you and the people you add
  • Save your journal entries, mood logs, and reflective prompt responses
  • Track your daily reading streak and show your weekly look-ahead
  • Provide customer support
  • Process subscriptions and manage your membership
  • Improve reading quality, tone safety, and app performance
  • Schedule kind, local notifications relevant to your streak (no server required)
Section 04

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

  • Birth data and journal entries stored on-device, encrypted, with optional private iCloud sync
  • Chart computation runs locally via an ephemeris library — your birth details don't need to leave your device to draw your chart
  • AI readings are generated server-side with strict tone and safety guardrails, and encrypted storage of account credentials
  • AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit

No system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We do, however, implement industry-standard practices to safeguard your data.

Section 05

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal or birth data. We may share data only:

  • With trusted service providers (e.g. our server infrastructure for content and reading generation, an AI provider for AI-written readings, RevenueCat for subscription management)
  • If required by applicable law
  • To protect the rights or safety of users or third parties

Your birth details and chart data are sent to our AI provider solely to generate your readings. They are not used for any other purpose.

Section 06

Account Deletion

You may request account deletion at any time by:

  • Using the in-app "Sign Out" option in Profile and contacting us to request deletion, or
  • Contacting us at support@astruneapp.com

When your account is deleted, your personal data, birth details, saved charts, and journal history will be permanently removed from our servers.

Section 07

Astrology & Reflection Disclaimer

Astrune provides astrology content for entertainment and self-reflection purposes. It is not medical, psychological, or financial advice, and it does not predict or guarantee future events.

We frame every reading around growth and reflection, never fatalism — always use your own judgement, and consult a qualified professional for medical, mental health, or financial decisions.

Section 08

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Section 09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our website. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice.
